A day in the bootcamp

Same rhythm, every day.

Predictable structure so students always know what's next — and most of the time is spent building, not watching.

0:00 – 0:20

Arrival & Setup

Attendance, laptop checks, tool setup, mentor help.

0:20 – 0:50

AI Showcase

Live demos and jaw-dropping examples. "Wait… AI can do THAT?"

0:50 – 1:40

Guided Workshop

Instructor teaches, students follow along step by step.

1:40 – 1:50

Break

Recharge. Snacks, questions, breathing room.

1:50 – 3:00

Build Session

Students build their own version. Mentors roam and help.

3:00 – 3:30

Showcase & Wrap

Share progress, ask questions, preview tomorrow.

After the bootcamp

The Summer Builder Challenge 🌞

The week is just the launchpad. All summer you keep building — with mentor support and an active Discord — then submit to the Showcase Portal. Pick your track:

📱 AI App

An app real people would use.

  • Study assistant
  • Chatbot
  • Productivity tool

🤖 AI Agent

Something that takes actions, not just chats.

  • Research agent
  • Coding agent
  • Planner agent

⚙️ AI Automation

Workflows that run themselves.

  • Email workflows
  • Data automation
  • Content generation

💡 Startup Idea

Turn a build into a business.

  • AI business tool
  • SaaS concept
  • Community project

How to submit

STEP 01

Use everything from the bootcamp. Lean on mentors and Discord when stuck.

STEP 02
